Help&Support

Need help using this banking system? Here is a quick guide

  • Create Account Fill In Costumer Name, Choose an account type, and set an initial deposit
  • Deposit Enter the amount and method (Cash or Bank Transfer) to fund an account
  • Withdraw Enter the amount you want to withdraw
  • Transfer: sends funds to another account number using the account number

if you encounter any errors or issues please check your form input or contact the system administrator
